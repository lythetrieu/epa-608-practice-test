What is the EPA 608 Test?

+

The EPA 608 test is a certification exam required under the Federal Clean Air Act for HVAC professionals and trainees. Our practice test helps you prepare by simulating the types of questions you'll see on the actual exam. Content is updated to reflect the 2026 AIM Act requirements governing refrigerant handling and phase-down schedules.

How many types of EPA 608 Certification are there?

+

There are four categories:

  • Core ‚Äì Required for all certifications.
  • Type I ‚Äì For servicing small appliances.
  • Type II ‚Äì For servicing or disposing of high-pressure appliances (except small appliances and MVACs).
  • Type III ‚Äì For servicing or disposing of low-pressure appliances.

Passing all three types earns you the Universal Certification.

Who offers the EPA 608 Certification Exam?

+

The ESCO Institute administers the official EPA 608 exam and related study materials. Many online platforms, including ours, offer free practice exams to help you prepare.
