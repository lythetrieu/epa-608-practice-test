How to Memorize These Numbers in 24 Hours

  • 10-20-30 Rule: Comfort = 10%, Commercial = 20%, IPR = 30%. Think "residential is strictest."
  • 15-10 Rule: Type III rupture disc = 15 psig. Max test pressure = 10 psig. Never confuse them.
  • 80-90 Rule: Type I passive recovery — 80% (working), 90% (broken).
  • Three Rs: Recovery (remove), Recycling (clean on-site), Reclamation (ARI 700 standard). Know the definitions cold.
  • Take the Core Practice Test, then review every wrong answer against this cheat sheet. Repeat until 85%+ consistent.
