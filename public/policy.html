Privacy Policy

EPA 608 Practice Test

Last Updated: August 10, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This app is designed with user control first — you decide what to share, and your study history is stored only on your device.

🔒 Quick Summary

  • No Login Required – All features are available without creating an account.
  • Local-Only Storage – Your scores and history are saved in your browser (localStorage).
  • You're in Control – If you clear your browser cookies/history, all stored data will be permanently erased.
  • Optional Contact – You can choose to share your email for updates, feedback, or feature notices — completely optional.

📝 Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the EPA 608 Practice Test web app ("we," "our," or "the Service") handles your information. Our goal is to provide an effective learning tool while letting you control your own data.

📊 Information We Collect

Automatically Stored on Your Device:

  • Test results, progress history, and settings are saved only in your browser.
  • Clearing cookies or browser history will erase all stored progress.

Information You Choose to Share:

  • If you use our optional contact form to request updates, give feedback, or ask questions, we will collect your name and email address.
  • This information is stored securely and used only for the purposes you choose.

🛡️ Data Protection

  • Local-Only Save – No test history or personal data is sent to our servers unless you choose to submit the contact form.
  • Encrypted Communication – All connections use HTTPS/TLS 1.3 for security.
  • Secure Storage for Contact Form Data – Any contact form submissions are stored in secure systems and never sold.

⏳ Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can erase all test history anytime by clearing cookies or local storage in your browser.
  • Contact form submissions can be deleted upon request — simply email us.

🤝 Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may display ads (including Mediavine) to support the free service. These ads may use cookies — you can control or disable them via your browser settings.

🔑 Your Privacy Rights

  • Full control to delete local data anytime by clearing your browser cookies/history.
  • Choice to opt in or out of update notices and feedback forms.

🍪 Cookie Policy

We use two types of cookies:

  • Essential local storage – to save your scores and preferences.
  • Advertising cookies – used by Mediavine or similar ad partners to serve relevant ads (you can opt out via your browser).

🔄 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Any significant changes will be posted prominently on the site.

📫 Contact Us

For privacy concerns, feedback, or data deletion requests:

Email: privacy@epa608practicetest.net
